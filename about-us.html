Kitfo

Finely chopped tender beef seasoned with mitmita spice and kibe (seasoned butter). Traditionally served raw, steak tartar style or cooked to your liking.

Photo by M.A.O.

Gomen

Collard greens slowly simmered in vegetable broth with finely chopped onions, garlic, ginger and other spices.

Photo by M.A.O.

Demera Beef Special

Tender beef marinated and stir fried with bell peppers, jalapeno, and a multitude of spices on a hot skillet with awaze and sanafich side dip served with a side salad.

Photo by M.A.O.

Doro Wot

A traditional dish of chicken leg slowly cooked in berbere spice sauce with garlic, onion, ginger, coriander, cardamom, kibe(seasoned butter), and a hardboiled egg.
